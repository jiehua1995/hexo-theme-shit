FAQ

Frequently questioned answers.

Is SHIT a real journal?

It is a real website for a fictional journal system with a clear satire notice.

Are the articles peer-reviewed?

They are reviewed by an imaginary editorial process involving at least one hostile but unavailable Reviewer 2.

Can I cite SHIT articles?

You can, but you should disclose context, genre, and your own standards of evidence.

Can I submit my own manuscript?

Yes. The default workflow routes submissions through GitHub Issues.

Are the references real?

Sometimes. Others are fictional, emotionally necessary, or generated upon reasonable hallucination.

Are AI-generated articles allowed?

Yes, when clearly labeled and not presented as professional advice.

What happens if an article accidentally becomes true?

It may be corrected, celebrated, or retracted depending on tone and timing.
