Introduction

This article is intentionally overloaded. It exists to test how far a SHIT article can stretch before the formatting begins to hallucinate independently.

It also acts as a regression page for future theme edits: when a card, equation, embed, or widget becomes visually strange, this article should complain first and loudly. In that sense it is less a manuscript than a carefully curated stress test wearing citation clothes.
